Photography

Most popular photography spots in Bali

Bali is a beautiful island located in Indonesia and is famous for its stunning landscapes, vibrant culture, and warm hospitality. It's no surprise that Bali is a popular destination for photographers looking to capture its beauty.

  • Tanah Lot Temple

    This temple is perched on a rocky outcrop overlooking the ocean, making it a popular spot for sunset photography.

  • Tegalalang Rice Terraces

    These rice terraces are a sight to behold, with their steep slopes and lush greenery. They are best photographed early in the morning or late in the afternoon.

  • Mount Batur

    This active volcano offers a stunning backdrop for photography, especially at sunrise when the sky turns shades of orange and pink.

  • Ubud Monkey Forest

    This forest is home to hundreds of playful monkeys, making it a popular spot for wildlife photography.

  • Uluwatu Temple

    This temple is located on a cliff overlooking the ocean and offers panoramic views of the coastline, especially at sunset.

  • Nusa Penida Island

    This island is home to some of Bali's most beautiful beaches and is perfect for capturing stunning ocean views and underwater photography.

  • Jatiluwih Rice Terraces

    These rice terraces are a UNESCO World Heritage Site and offer some of the most picturesque views in Bali.

  • Sekumpul Waterfall

    This waterfall is located in northern Bali and offers a breathtaking view of cascading waterfalls amidst lush greenery.

  • Pura Ulun Danu Bratan

    This temple is located on the shores of Lake Bratan and offers a unique opportunity for photography with its reflection in the water.
